# AI Support Cost Calculator

A free, no-signup calculator that shows what you actually pay **per AI-resolved support ticket** across the major tools (Intercom Fin, Zendesk AI, [Convot](https://convot.io)).

Most AI support tools bill *per resolution* — plug in your monthly volume and deflection rate, edit the per-resolution prices to match your quote, and see your true annual cost + how much is avoidable.

**Live:** https://tarang8811.github.io/ai-support-cost-calculator/

## Why it exists
"Per resolution" pricing sounds cheap at $0.99 — until you multiply by 12 months of volume. This tool makes the real number obvious.

## Built by
[Convot](https://convot.io) — modern, grounded AI customer support that doesn't tax you per ticket (free until $1k MRR).

## Notes
- 100% static (single `index.html`), no tracking, no backend.
- Prices are editable list-price estimates — put in your own numbers.
- MIT licensed — fork it, embed it, remix it.
