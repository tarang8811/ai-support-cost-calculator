# Free Support Calculators

Three free, no-signup calculators for anyone running (or pricing) customer support:

| Tool | What it answers |
|---|---|
| [**AI Support Cost**](https://tarang8811.github.io/ai-support-cost-calculator/) | What you actually pay *per AI-resolved ticket* across Intercom Fin, Zendesk AI and [Convot](https://convot.io) |
| [**Cost of Slow Support**](https://tarang8811.github.io/ai-support-cost-calculator/cost-of-slow-support.html) | The annual revenue you lose to slow first-response times |
| [**AI Support Savings**](https://tarang8811.github.io/ai-support-cost-calculator/ai-savings.html) | The agent hours, FTEs and dollars AI deflection frees up |

Most AI support tools bill *per resolution* — at $0.99 that sounds cheap until you multiply by a year of volume. These tools make the real numbers obvious. Every input is editable, so put in your own quote.

## Built by
[Convot](https://convot.io) — modern, grounded AI customer support that doesn't tax you per ticket (free until $1k MRR).

## Notes
- 100% static (plain HTML/CSS/JS), no tracking, no backend.
- All prices/assumptions are editable estimates — use your own numbers.
- MIT licensed — fork it, embed it, remix it.
